Back while I was studying at PUP, one of our activities were to create our very own game, but with the challenge that we had to create something that would all group members to be able to play. We ended up getting grouped by 6 members and after much discussion we decided to create an infinite platformer, as that is the only genre that could solve such a problem.

In this project, we used GameMaker and its drag-and-drop coding language to easily create a game of our own. I was in charge of coding the project, while the others created their own characters that were used in-game.

Unfortunately, I have also lost any footage of the game running as of now due to a multitude of issues. However, I have kept our documentation intact and below you'll find our storyboard and the assets we used in the game.
