In the times of the pandemic, fighting game tournaments could not be held due to unfortunate rise of the COVID-19 virus. This led to sites that usually provided shelter for the FGC (fighting game community) like Facebook, Reddit and Discord to be out of service or be completely filled in the case of some communities due to unexpected traffic from gamers that stayed at home.

My team and I came up with the idea of BATTLEForge in these difficult times. A forum website where people could leave links to their own communities and also engage with discussion about their favorite fighting games. The website was made with the current trending games of that year and we planned to add more eventually.

Since the website was just a group project and due to the quick responses by the aforementioned social media sites, the project could not progress any further. Some screenshots of the website in action can be found below.
